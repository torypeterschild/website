Dream Bot

@dreamy_bot_

Dream Bot searches for tweets that contain "I dreamt that." It then filters for mention of waking up, mention of it being a dream, anything extremely offensive, and tweets whatever comes after "I dream that."

Love in the Time Of Bot

@LoffInTheTimeOf

Love In The Time Of scrapes headlines from the Google News RSS feed and then uses TextBlob to extract noun phrases (somewhat imperfectly). It filters for very offensive content and very common names, and selects randomly from the phrases that remain.

Follow @LoffInTheTimeOf
